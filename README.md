# Sales Data Analysis using Pandas & Matplotlib

## Overview

This project analyzes a sales dataset using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib. The objective is to perform data analysis, generate business insights, and visualize sales trends through different types of charts.

---

## Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains information such as:

- Month
- Product
- Category
- Units Sold
- Price
- Revenue

---

## Project Tasks

- Data exploration
- Revenue calculation
- Monthly revenue analysis
- Product-wise revenue analysis
- Category-wise analysis
- Highest and lowest revenue products
- Average revenue
- Data visualization

---

## Visualizations

- Line Chart – Month vs Revenue
- Bar Chart – Product vs Revenue
- Pie Chart – Revenue Share by Product
- Histogram – Revenue Distribution
- Scatter Plot – Units Sold vs Revenue

---

## Key Insights

- Identified the highest revenue generating product.
- Compared monthly revenue performance.
- Analyzed product-wise sales contribution.
- Visualized revenue distribution.
- Studied the relationship between units sold and revenue.
